Why was this document written?

Why was this document written? Because we always end up with a huge amount of shceduled cronjobs, that no-one knows what they do and why they do it. Even worse, sometimes they keep on running for days, in a bugged state populating folders and consuming huge amount of resources.

Well ok ... by the way, it's a good opportunity to experiment with docbook ...

Audience

Anyone who uses the bpe account on vincenty should read this. The admin should make sure of that!

Chapter 1. The bpe2 Account

Abstract

In this chapter we will introduce the bpe2 account; its directory tree, scope, vital folders and programs, what it is supposed to be doing etc...

The account, is responsible for and should be doing the following:

  • Download various GNSS products and data,

  • Process GNSS networks on a routine basis,

  • Analyze GNSS stations time-series files

  • Upload various information to other servers for presentation.

The account's directory tree and cron table should accomodate all of the above.
